## Extracted quantitative anchors

1. `IRON AND STEEL` section (`PDF p.106-107`)
- U.S. raw steel production is listed at `82 Mt` for 2025 (`79.5 Mt` in 2024).
- Combined U.S. raw steel production capacity is listed at `~105 Mt/year` in 2025.
- World raw steel production table (`2025e`) reports `~1,900 Mt` total, including `China ~980 Mt`.

2. `IRON ORE` section (`PDF p.112`)
- U.S. iron ore production is listed at `38,000 kt` (usable ore) in 2025 (`45,100 kt` in 2024).
- U.S. iron ore exports are listed at `7,800 kt` in 2025 (`10,500 kt` in 2024).
- The section lists four U.S. metallic plants operating in 2025: one DRI plant and three HBI plants.

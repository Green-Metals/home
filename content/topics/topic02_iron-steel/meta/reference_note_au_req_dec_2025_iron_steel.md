## Extracted quantitative anchors

1. Australia export scale and iron ore share (`PDF p.8`)
- REQ states iron ore export earnings continue to account for `over 25%` of Australia's resource and energy export earnings.
- Iron ore export earnings are forecast at `A$114 billion` (2025-26) and `A$107 billion` (2026-27) from `A$116.4 billion` (2024-25).

2. Iron ore market and outlook (`PDF p.21-22`)
- Iron ore FOB price outlook: `US$87/t` (2025), `US$85/t` (2026), `US$82/t` (2027), from `US$93/t` (2024).
- Global steel output in first 10 months of 2025 is reported at `1.52 billion tonnes`, down `1.9%` year-on-year.
- Australia's iron ore export volumes are reported up `2.8%` year-on-year to `233 Mt` in Sep quarter 2025.
- Export volumes are forecast to reach about `1,007 Mt` by `2026-27`.
